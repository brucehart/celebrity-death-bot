Privacy Policy — CelebrityDeathBot

Effective date: August 16, 2025

CelebrityDeathBot (“the Bot”) sends Telegram updates when a celebrity dies. We designed the Bot to collect as little information as possible.

  • We only store your numeric Telegram user ID.
  • We use it solely to send you the updates you requested.
  • We do not store your name, username, phone number, profile photo, message contents, or other personal data.
  • We do not sell or share your data for advertising.

Data We Collect

Telegram user ID (numeric): Stored to manage your subscription and deliver notifications.

That’s it. We do not intentionally collect or store any other information.

How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver notifications: Use your user ID to send updates through Telegram.
  • Manage subscription state: Determine whether you are subscribed or unsubscribed.

Legal Basis (EEA/UK users)

We process your user ID based on your request/consent to receive messages and/or our legitimate interests in providing the service you asked for.

Data Retention

  • We keep your user ID while you are subscribed.
  • If you unsubscribe (e.g., by sending /stop) or request deletion, we delete your user ID from our system.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your user ID with third parties, except:
    • Service providers strictly as needed to run the Bot (e.g., hosting/infrastructure). They are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
    • Legal compliance if required by applicable law.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your user ID against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

International Transfers

Your user ID may be processed in the country where our infrastructure is located. We use reputable providers and apply safeguards appropriate to the transfer.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or object to processing/withdraw consent.

How to exercise: Send /stop in the Bot to unsubscribe (removes your user ID from the active list), or contact us (see below) and include your numeric Telegram user ID so we can locate and remove it.

Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by children under 13 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect operational or legal changes. We’ll update the Effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the Bot.
